About

School attendance boundaries determine which school a child attends based on where they live. In Chicago, many boundaries run along highways and rail lines — physical barriers that were often routed through Black neighborhoods in the mid-20th century.

The Boundary Segregation Index (BSI) is a weighted composite: 40% racial composition gap, 30% low-income gap, 30% test-proficiency gap between the two schools on either side of a shared boundary. Click any boundary, or a name from the list above, to compare the two schools.

Data: NCES SABINS · CPS Open Data · Census 2020 · Illinois ISBE Report Card
